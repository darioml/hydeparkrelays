Peak Fitness

Reach your peak fitness by following the high quality workouts and professional exercise advice in Peak.

The Peak Fitness System contains full body, circuit style workouts with progressive difficulty that cater for all fitness levels and help you advance. All workouts are created by Personal Trainer and international level athlete, Ian Williamson. They have been tuned over many years of training clients with a range of fitness levels and are designed to give you a challenging workout whilst avoiding injury.

Peak also contains full instructive videos for more than 80 exercises. Each video explains how to perform the exercise in detail, covering good form and typical mistakes to avoid.

Follow each workout using the Peak workout player. Supporting videos for each exercise provide a reminder of the correct form as you go through the workout.

All this, and it’s completely free.
