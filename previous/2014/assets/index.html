What and where?

The Hyde Park Relays is a student relay race (organised by the Imperial College Cross Country & Athletics club) through Hyde Park and Kensington Gardens, winding around the Serpentine lake and down to Hyde Park Corner (for the men). The race brings together running clubs, societies and student from the UK and across Europe.

Important Course change: Due to events out of our control, we are unable to use the classic route around the Serpentine for this year's race. We were only notified of this issue two weeks before the race date, having previously confirmed that we had full use of the park on the 15th.

We realise that this news may cause you disappointment, as we have had ourselves, and we apologise for this. However we have endeavoured to produce an alternative route of equal quality and distance which we are sure you will enjoy.

Thank you for your understanding.

Men's teams run a total of 6 laps, with each runner completing a lap of not less than 5.00 km.

Women's teams run a total of 4 laps, with each runner completing a lap of not less than 3.21 km.

On the day

10:30–13:00
Team registration in Metric in the Union. Find the form in Metric and fill it in with the names of your runners (1 form per team). You'll then receive your numbers, and have the chance to buy a race T-shirt.

14:00
Start of men's/mixed race.

14:05
Start of women's race.

17:00
Food and drink served in the Senior Common Room (SCR) on the main walkway.

18:30
Presentations and prizes (in the Senior Common Room), after which you can clean up and make yourself look pretty! Showers in Ethos, Imperial College Sports Centre.

20:00–02:00
Hyde Park Relays after party in Metric.
