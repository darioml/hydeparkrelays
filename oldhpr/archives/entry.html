How to Enter a Team!


Contact hpr@imperial.ac.uk to enter.
Further details will be soon posted to this website!
^ Top

Eligibility

Teams must be entirely of provable students. However each college may enter one guest team, which will not be eligible for prizes, although any students within the team will be eligible for individual prizes.

Imperial College teams may be comprised of students, staff, and may be mixed.

^ Top

The rest...

The entry cost also includes free entry to the after race party in the Union. Foreign teams must pay on the day of the race as we cannot use foreign cheques but please send the entry forms in anyway.

NOTE: THE COURSE IS ON HARD SURFACES, SO NO SPIKES ARE ALLOWED. ^ Top
