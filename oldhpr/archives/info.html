The Event

The Hyde Park Relays is a student relay race through Hyde Park and Kensington Gardens, winding around the Serpentine lake and down to Hyde Park Corner (for the men). Please see the map for the exact route.


As always exclusive race T-shirts will also available on the race day!

^ Top

How long is it?

Race structure:

Mens teams run a total of 6 laps, with each runner completing a lap of not less than 5.164 km. (approx 3.2m)

Womens teams run a total of 4 laps, with each runner completing a lap of not less than 3.464 km. (approx 2.2m)

Note: These distances have been officially measured.

Food and drink

Food and drink will be provided before the prize giving in the JCR on the main walkway. Please see the timetable for the schedule.

Accommodation

Accommodation Arrangements:

Free accommodation can be provided in the form of floor space in Southside Gym and/or Union Gym for Thursday to Sunday. Please indicate on the entry form which (if any) nights you want to stay. People who requested accommodation will have space to sleep from 22:00 to 09:00 the following morning, when the rooms must be vacated. Note that in the event of too many people wanting to stay on Saturday night we may ask for a very small charge to cover extra hired accommodation or in extreme circumstances may limit free accommodation to foreign teams. To guarantee accommodation please return completed entry forms as soon as possible.

Showers and changing rooms

Showers and Changing rooms will be provided on the day, but currently precise locations have not be confirmed. You can be assured they will be clearly sign-posted on the day.
