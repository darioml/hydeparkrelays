eligibility
Teams must be entirely of provable students. However each college may enter one guest team, which will not be eligible for Fila prizes, although any students within the team will be eligible for individual prizes.

prizes
This year's event is part of the Fila Grand Prix of University Relays and they are assisting us with £1000 worth of Fila prizes to be awarded to the: 

1st, 2nd, 3rd team – current students (Men & Women)
1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th and 5th fastest individual legs of the day – current students (Men & Women)

Separate Prizes will be awarded to:
1st Foreign team – not in the overall top three (Men & Women)
1st Guest Team (Men & Women)
1st Internal Imperial College Team (Men & Women)

We will also be presenting the Gary Hoare trophies (to the slowest teams) as winning isn't all that matters!

and the rest...
