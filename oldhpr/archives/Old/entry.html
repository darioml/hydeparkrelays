Eligibility
Teams must be entirely of provable students. However each college may enter one guest team, which will not be eligible for prizes, although any students within the team will be eligible for individual prizes.

Imperial Collge teams may be comprised of students, staff, and may be mixed.


Prizes
1st, 2nd, 3rd team – current students (Men & Women)
1st, 2nd, 3rd, 4th and 5th fastest individual legs of the day – current students (Men & Women)

Separate Prizes will be awarded to:
1st Foreign team – not in the overall top three (Men & Women)
1st Guest Team (Men & Women)
1st Internal Imperial College Team (Men & Women)

We will also be presenting the Gary Hoare trophies (to the slowest teams) as winning isn't all that matters!


And the rest...
The entry cost also includes free entry to the after race party in the Union. Foreign teams must pay on the day of the race as we cannot use foreign cheques but please send the entry forms in anyway.

You may have noticed that we have had to increase the entry price by a few pounds from previous years, this is because we are now having to pay a substantial charge for the use of Hyde Park. However we are confident that this will be the last increase for a number of years, and we still feel it offers excellent value for money.
