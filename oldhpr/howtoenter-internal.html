HOW TO ENTER

6 simple steps to entering your team for the Relays on 26th Feb 2011:

1. Gather a group of friends from your class, sports club or society, just as long as they're Imperial through and through (6 for a men's team (£24), 4 for a women's team (£16), or 6 for a mixed team (£24) in any combination). You will note this is much cheaper than for external teams!

2. Give yourselves a name

3. Fill in this form: HPR Internal

4. Email it to hpr@imperial.ac.uk with the subject heading 'INTERNAL ENTRY'.

5. Click here to buy your ticket online from the union website!

6. Pick up your race numbers from DB's between 10:30 and 12:30 on 26/02/2011

Note: There'll be excellent prizes for the best and some of the worst internal teams in each category! The entry cost also includes a free meal after the race in the SCR and free entry to the race after party in the Union.
